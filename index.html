Frequently Asked Questions
How do I request land through Bhorosha?

Navigate to the "Land Requests" section, fill in the details about your land needs, and submit the request. Our platform will notify you when matching lands are available.

Can I save land listings to review later?

Yes, simply tap the bookmark icon on any land listing to save it. Access all your saved lands anytime in the "Saved Lands" section.

What units are supported in the Land Converter?

The converter supports Bigha, Katha, and Acre — the common land units used across Bangladesh.

How do I contact landowners directly?

Once you find a suitable listing, you can contact the landowner through the platform’s secure messaging system.
