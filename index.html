Frequently Asked Questions
What is Bhorosha?

Bhorosha is a digital platform that connects landowners with capable farmers and agripreneurs who want to lease agricultural land. We help unlock idle land’s potential while ensuring safe, profitable agreements for both parties.

Who can list land on Bhorosha?

Any verified landowner or representative with cultivable land in Bangladesh can list it on our platform. We assist with documentation and ensure listings are secure and trustworthy.

I want to lease land for farming. How do I start?

Simply sign up as a leaser, browse available listings by region or preference, and send a request to the landowner. You’ll be guided through negotiation, verification, and final agreement steps.

Is it safe to deal through Bhorosha?

Yes. We offer deal templates, ownership verification tools, and a secure channel for communication. Bhorosha ensures transparency and reduces risks for both landowners and leasers.

Does Bhorosha take a commission?

Yes, we charge a small service fee only when a successful deal is finalized. There is no charge to create an account or browse land listings.

Can I list land if I live abroad or in another city?

Absolutely! Bhorosha is built for absentee landowners. You can manage listings, communicate with leasers, and finalize deals remotely through our web platform.

What kind of land can I lease on Bhorosha?

Primarily cultivable agricultural land. It must be legal, owned (or legally leased by you), and suitable for farming or related agri-projects.

What if a deal goes wrong?

Bhorosha provides basic dispute resolution support, ratings, and documentation tools. While we are not a legal authority, we help you stay protected with templates and clear communication.

Is the platform available on mobile?

Yes. Bhorosha is fully mobile-optimized and works on both smartphones and desktops. A dedicated app is also in development.
